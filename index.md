## What ViGos Gallery does

ViGos Gallery is a local media gallery app for Android. It reads photos, videos, and audio files stored on your device. No media files are uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server.

---

## Data we collect

### Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics)
When the app crashes, an anonymous crash report is sent to Google Firebase. This includes device model, Android version, and the crash stack trace. It does not include your name, media files, or personal data.

### Usage analytics (Firebase Analytics)
We collect anonymous usage events (e.g. which features are used, how often). No personally identifiable information is included.

You can opt out of analytics by disabling "Usage & diagnostics" in your Android device settings.

---

## Data we do NOT collect

- Your photos, videos, or audio files
- Your name, email, or location
- Any data that leaves your device beyond crash reports and anonymous analytics
